## What is EverWeb?

![EverWeb Design View](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSyMeXRIZlyIa6iej9SgDLWFamEnkLo5RcdcQ&s)

EverWeb is a professional-grade website design tool that allows users to create responsive, modern websites without writing a single line of code. It combines an easy-to-use drag-and-drop editor with advanced features such as custom HTML widgets, SEO management, and integrated e-commerce functionality.

Whether you are building a personal blog, a business site, or an online store, EverWeb provides all the tools needed to design, customize, and publish your website directly to your hosting provider. The software is fully optimized for macOS, ensuring smooth performance on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs. With regular updates and a user-friendly interface, EverWeb is trusted by designers, educators, and entrepreneurs worldwide.

The application supports a variety of third-party integrations, including Google Fonts, YouTube embeds, and contact forms. You can also add interactive elements like image sliders, maps, and social media feeds with just a few clicks. EverWeb’s flexible layout system ensures your site looks great on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

- macOS 10.14 Mojave or newer  
- Intel or Apple Silicon Mac  
- 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)  
- 500 MB free disk space
